Away (feat. Beenzino) - Primary

The soft piano licks combined with the heavy bass create a sensual mood for the listener. The rap flows along with the relaxing melody leading up to the ambient chorus that results in a easy to listen to song. Primary is known for their korean hip hop music with a touch of classical beats, whereas Beenzino is renown for his distinct voice and rap style.

Spoiler - Epik High

‘Spoiler’ can be described as soothing and talented rapping that complement the poignant and somewhat dramatic melody. Epik High tracks are always deep and make you think. Their music seems to pick apart the human mind and seem to understand how to verbalize emotions.

Tomorrow - BTS

‘Tomorrow’ draws you in with the heavy atmospheric beats combined with different repetitive sounds heard in the instrumental. ‘Tomorrow’ features three different rap styles complemented by four different vocalists for the chorus. The different instruments contrasts against the fast paced beats in combination with a slow background piano result in an addicting melody.
